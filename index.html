01 · Virginia Data Center Inventory

Map the portfolio, building by building.

Each of the 412 sites combines publicly sourced location and floor-area data with a modeled HVAC profile, creating a high-fidelity foundation for simulating how building choices affect performance and for guiding more informed decisions across the portfolio.

02 · FilterStudio

Select the right filters for your building.

Filtration is not just an IAQ decision; in data centers, it is an energy, equipment, climate, public health, and lifecycle cost decision. FilterStudio translates lab-reported filter performance into real building conditions, modeling how removal efficiency, dust loading, pressure drop, fan energy, and downstream co-benefits evolve over time. The result is a clearer view of which filters deliver the best total value for a specific facility.

03 · MATRIX

SIMULATE BEFORE YOU DECIDE.

Operational and material choices can reshape a data center’s performance long before they show up in utility bills, maintenance records, or occupant experience. MATRIX is 9F’s physics-based simulation engine for rapidly testing those choices before they are implemented, using building-specific inputs and real-time environmental data where available to quantify how thousands of scenarios perform under real building conditions.

    04 · H.E.A.A.L.

    VERIFY HOW YOUR BUILDING IS ACTUALLY PERFORMING

    Models are most powerful when they are connected to measured conditions. H.E.A.A.L. turns real-time indoor environmental data into health-based building intelligence, helping teams see whether data center conditions are aligned with science-based thresholds and where operational changes may be needed to protect occupants and equipment alike. That means a lower-touch way to track building health, support transparency, and verify that efficiency decisions are not creating hidden risks.
