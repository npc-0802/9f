01 · Why it matters

Data centers are a high-stakes building type.

The healthy-buildings framework — that the air inside a space drives the health and performance of what happens in it — applies to server halls as forcefully as it does to offices and schools. The economics just look different.

06 · Product 03 — H.E.A.A.L.

Watch the air. Score the building.

Modeling tells you what should happen. H.E.A.A.L. tells you what is. Low-cost IAQ sensors stream temperature, humidity, CO₂, and particulate matter — every metric matters twice: once for the data-processing equipment that needs stable operating conditions, and once for the people who work in and around the building. Operators see drift before it becomes downtime; the same readings keep indoor environments inside healthy-building thresholds.

Energy deltas, translated to human and climate cost.

That converts a percentage point of energy savings into the language operators, regulators, and communities all share: dollars, lives, and emissions.
