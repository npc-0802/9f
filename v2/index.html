How this is calculated +

These are modeled co-benefits, not invoiced totals. The $39.9M figure aggregates energy-cost, climate-damage, and public-health-damage line items across the 412-site Virginia portfolio using filter-comparison energy deltas from FilterStudio.

Climate valuation
Harvard CoBE per-MWh climate factor · derived from the social cost of carbon at the EPA-style central rate. Energy delta × per-MWh factor = climate damages avoided.
Public-health valuation
CoBE per-MWh health factor · PM2.5-attributable morbidity and mortality valued at a value-of-statistical-life (VSL) basis consistent with the source framework.
Energy cost
Energy delta × $0.18 / kWh (Virginia wholesale-adjacent reference rate). Site-specific tariffs will deviate; the figure is a portfolio-aggregate proxy, not a procurement quote.
Per-site variance
Per-site results vary by eGRID region (RFCE / SRVC for the modeled portfolio), outdoor PM2.5 baseline, system type (DLC / DEC), and ventilation/recirculation split. The statewide total smooths across this distribution.
Scope of claim
Modeled against the 412-site Virginia inventory using a single filter-pair comparison (Filter A vs Filter B) per site, holding all other building parameters at their inventory values. Cross-market extrapolation requires re-parameterization.
